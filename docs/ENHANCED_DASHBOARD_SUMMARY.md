## 🎨 **Enhanced Dashboard Features**

### **1. Advanced KPI Cards (Top Row)**

#### **Weekly Revenue Card**
- Shows 7-day revenue total
- Week-over-week comparison with percentage change
- Comparison text: "vs last week: ₱X"
- Trend: "X transactions today"
- **Sparkline**: 7-day revenue mini-chart
- Trend indicator icon (up/down arrow)

#### **Service Completion Rate Card**
- Shows completion percentage
- Compares to 75% target benchmark
- Comparison: "X of Y jobs"
- Trend: "Avg time: 2.5 days"
- Color-coded trend badge (green/red)

#### **Active Pipeline Card**
- Shows pending + ongoing count
- Percentage change indicator
- Comparison: "X pending, Y ongoing"
- Trend: "Z completed"

#### **Inventory Health Card**
- Shows healthy stock percentage
- Negative change for low stock items
- Comparison: "X items need restocking"
- Trend: "Y total parts"
- **Sparkline**: 7-day parts usage trend
